## Approval Authority

Travel expenses are approved based on the total estimated trip cost and destination type.

### Domestic Travel
- Up to INR 25,000: Auto-approved (no additional approval needed)
- INR 25,001 to INR 100,000: Direct manager approval
- INR 100,001 to INR 500,000: VP approval
- Above INR 500,000: CXO / CFO approval

### International Travel
- Up to INR 50,000 (or equivalent): Auto-approved
- INR 50,001 to INR 200,000: Direct manager approval
- INR 200,001 to INR 1,000,000: VP approval
- Above INR 1,000,000: CXO / CFO approval

### High-Risk Destinations
- All travel to high-risk destinations requires manager approval regardless of cost
- Up to INR 50,000: Manager approval
- INR 50,001 to INR 200,000: VP approval
- Above INR 200,000: CXO / CFO approval

## High-Risk Countries List

The following countries are classified as high-risk and require additional approvals and security briefings:
- Afghanistan, Iraq, Syria, Yemen, Libya, Somalia
- Parts of: Pakistan, Nigeria, Colombia, Venezuela

This list is updated quarterly by the Corporate Security team.

## Multi-City Trips

For trips involving multiple cities or countries, the total trip cost should be used for approval thresholds, not individual segment costs.

## Emergency Travel

In case of genuine emergencies, travel can be booked without pre-approval. However, a retrospective approval must be obtained within 48 hours of booking.
